About AJ Supermart

Founded in 2023, AJ Supermart is a family-owned grocery store committed to providing fresh, high-quality products at affordable prices.

Our Mission

To make healthy and fresh groceries accessible to everyone in our community while maintaining exceptional customer service.

Our Values

  • Quality products at fair prices
  • Friendly and knowledgeable staff
  • Supporting local farmers and producers
  • Sustainable business practices
